# Product Scope

## Marketplace Areas

- Vehicles: cars, motorcycles, commercial vehicles, parts, and dealer listings.
- Real estate: apartments, houses, commercial property, rent, sale, new buildings.
- Goods: consumer goods, electronics, furniture, fashion, hobby items, and services.

## Early User Roles

- Visitor: searches, filters, compares, and views listing details.
- Registered user: saves listings, chats, creates listings, manages profile.
- Seller: promotes listings and manages inventory.
- Moderator: reviews reports, suspicious listings, and category quality.
- Admin: manages categories, locations, users, billing, and platform settings.

## MVP Priorities

1. Authentication and profiles.
2. Category and location taxonomy.
3. Listing creation, search, and detail pages.
4. Image uploads and listing moderation workflow.
5. Saved listings and seller contact flow.
